Privacy Policy — Tiktok Clips

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tiktok Clips application ("the App") handles user data.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects and stores only the following data locally on your device:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens — used to authenticate your TikTok account for video uploads. Stored locally on your device.
  • Video metadata — titles, descriptions, tags, and upload status of your videos. Stored locally in JSON files.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
  • We do not transmit any data to third-party servers other than TikTok's official API.

4. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok API on your behalf.
  • Upload video content to your TikTok account.
  • Track which videos have been uploaded to avoid duplicates.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers. You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the token files and JSON metadata files.

6. Third-Party Services

The App communicates only with TikTok's official API. Please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy for information on how TikTok handles your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer at this website.
